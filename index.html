About Me — Lavanya Naithani

Precision in Every Stitch | Vision Beyond Fashion

Hello! I am Lavanya Naithani, an entrepreneur and garment manufacturer hailing from the picturesque state of Uttarakhand, India. With over a decade of experience in the textile industry, I have dedicated my career to redefining quality and innovation in garment manufacturing. As the Director of Ethenic Attire India Pvt. Ltd., I have had the privilege of working with global clients to deliver world-class garments that blend tradition and modernity seamlessly.

My journey began with a deep-rooted passion for textiles and a keen eye for detail. Growing up in a vibrant cultural environment, I was always fascinated by the intricate craftsmanship and storytelling that textiles encapsulate. This fascination grew into a mission to create garments that not only meet global standards but also reflect the rich heritage of craftsmanship we are so proud of.

Since founding Ethenic Attire in 2013, I have been at the forefront of integrating cutting-edge technology with traditional techniques. Our manufacturing processes are built on precision, sustainability, and ethical practices, ensuring that every garment we produce is a testament to our commitment to quality and responsibility. I strongly believe that fashion has the power to inspire and empower, and it is our responsibility to do so while respecting our environment and communities.

Beyond my professional life, I am an advocate for sustainability and mindful consumption in the fashion industry. I actively participate in industry seminars and workshops, sharing insights and encouraging young entrepreneurs to think innovatively while staying true to their values. I am particularly passionate about mentoring aspiring professionals who are eager to make a mark in the textile and garment sector.

When I’m not in the factory or attending conferences, I enjoy exploring new cultures and cuisines, which often serve as an endless source of inspiration for my work. Married to Taniya, who shares my passion for creativity and enterprise, we strive to build a balanced life filled with purpose, passion, and positivity.

My vision is to continue pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in garment manufacturing while staying grounded in the principles of sustainability, craftsmanship, and innovation. Whether creating bespoke garments for a luxury brand or crafting large-scale orders for global clients, my goal remains the same: to deliver excellence in every piece.

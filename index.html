About Me — Lavanya Naithani

Precision in Every Stitch | Vision Beyond Fashion

Born and raised in Uttarakhand, Lavanya Naithani is a seasoned entrepreneur and garment manufacturer. Since 2013, he has been redefining quality in the textile industry as the Director of Ethenic Attire India Pvt. Ltd. His journey is marked by innovation, precision, and a commitment to excellence.

Lavanya's work reflects a seamless blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern technology. He is married to Taniya, and they share a vision of balancing creativity and enterprise. Together, they inspire a team dedicated to delivering the finest garments to clients worldwide.

With a focus on sustainability and ethical practices, Lavanya believes in creating fashion that empowers individuals and communities while preserving the environment.
